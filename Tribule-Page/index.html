The King who loves all

Timeline

  • 6BC approx. Jesus’ birth

    Jesus was born inside a manger in Bethlehem. There was no other rooms in the inns so they had to stay inside a little farm area. Mary, his mom, along with her husband Joseph, had birthed Jesus there surrounded by the farm animals. A bright star appeared in the sky. People saw it and knew something special had happened.

  • 4 BC approx. Wise men visit and Jesus escapes to Egypt

    This is the time when the wise men brought there gifts to give to the new king who was born. They brought three gifts: gold, frankincense, and myrrh.

  • 8 AD approx. Jesus’ childhood – left behind in Jerusalem

    This was the time when Mary and Joseph went back to Jerusalem after the ones who sought to destroy the king had passed on. This was the time when Jesus was in the temple speaking to the elders. They were astonished at his doctrine.

  • 28 AD approx. Jesus is baptized and tempted by the devil

    This is the time when Jesus had gone to elias (John the baptist) and asked to be baptized by him. Elias did so and the heavens opened and a voice came out saying, "This is my beloved son. Whom I love and am well pleased.", two doves came down and Jesus had obtained the holy spirit. Jesus had left for 40 days and the devil had tempted him. Jesus stayed true to his father.

  • 28 AD approx. Jesus is baptized and tempted by the devil

    Jesus gathers his 12 disciples to follow and see the wonderful miracles that God can do. They are amazed and glorify God. They are take up their cross and follow Jesus.

  • 28AD – 30AD approx. Jesus begins teaching and miraculous healings

    Jesus is one with the Father in heaven and teaches his people and shows them who the one true God is. Jesus heals the deaf to hear, the dumb to speak, paralyzed to walk, and even the dead to rise. All in his father's name, which is the God of David, God of Jacob, and the God of Israel.

  • Spring 30 AD approx. Jesus’ arrest, crucifixion and resurrection

    The scribes and the pharisees see the wonders and miracles Jesus does and get jealous. They get him arrested for blasphemy and when he was decided what to do with amongst the people. The scribes and pharisee turned the people against and they had sought to destroy and have him killed. They crucify him in Golgotha (which is a place of bones).

  • 3 days after the crucifixion

    On the third day Jesus had risen and met with his disciples in Galilee. He had shown them his doctrine was true. He returned to his father which is in heaven.
